About Me

“All children are artists. The problem is how to remain an artist once he grows up.” a quote by artist Pablo. I too started out as an artist as a child, won competitions every year for the best artist; as I grew up, design (front-end, interaction design) is my way of remaining an artist.
Eversince I was a child, I have been passionate about design; of course, it was just sketches & paintings then. Later, I was introduced to computers and the interesting ways it aided design. The more I worked with computers, the more I was curious to understand how it worked. My love for computers developed when I was in college where I was exposed to programming. I can still recall the sense of achievement I felt when I developed my first webpage.
